## Task

## Editing Checklist

### Language Polish
- Fix any awkward phrasing
- Smooth transitions between paragraphs
- Vary sentence length and structure
- Ensure consistent voice throughout

### AI Pattern Removal
Remove or rephrase any instances of:
- "It's important to note..."
- "In today's [anything]..."
- "Let's explore..."
- "At the end of the day..."
- Any phrase that sounds formulaic

### Clinical Accuracy
- Verify no clinical misinformation
- Ensure advice is appropriate for general audience
- Check that complex concepts are explained accessibly

### Voice Consistency
- Maintain warm, conversational tone
- Keep first-person perspective
- Preserve the therapist's authentic voice

### Quality Improvements
- Strengthen weak openings
- Punch up conclusions
- Ensure quotes are integrated naturally
- Verify word count is ~750 (+/- 30)

## Important Rules

- Do NOT add new content
- Do NOT remove key information
- Do NOT change the overall structure
- ONLY polish the existing prose
